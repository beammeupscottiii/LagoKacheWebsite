Welcome to Lago Kache, a taste of Ayiti in the heart of Brooklyn. We are not just a restaurant, but a focal point in our community, making space for celebration large and small. Whether you are craving our signature Griot, celebrating a milestone, or settling in for an evening of Live Haitian Mizik, an evening at Lago Kache will connect you with our culture.

Authentic Haitian Cuisine

Dive into the bold flavors of the Caribbean with our traditional dishes, featuring savory Griot, rich Legume, and our signature Diri Djon Djon.

Specialty Beverages

Savour our collection of aged rums and bespoke cocktails, perfectly balancing Caribbean tradition with modern mixology

Elegant Private Parties

Celebrate your milestones in style. Our versatile and sophisticated venue is the perfect backdrop for birthdays, weddings, and exclusive gatherings.
